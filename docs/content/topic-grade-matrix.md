# Topic-by-Grade Matrix

## Grades and Topics

| Grade | Topics |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Number recognition, counting, addition, subtraction |
| 2 | Number recognition, counting, addition, subtraction, comparison, word problems |
| 3 | Addition, subtraction, comparison, word problems |

## Difficulty Rules

| Topic | Easy | Medium | Hard |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Number recognition | 1-10 | 11-20 | 21-30 |
| Counting | 1-4 objects | 5-8 objects | 9-12 objects |
| Addition | sums up to 5-8 | sums up to 15 | sums up to 30 |
| Subtraction | answers up to 5 | answers up to 12 | answers up to 20+ |
| Comparison | small pair gaps | mixed pair gaps | larger values and mixed patterns |
| Word problems | one-step simple | one-step with larger values | one-step with higher values and mixed contexts |
