# Early Feedback Collection Process

## Goal

Collect lightweight parent and teacher feedback after the first limited release.

## Method

- Share the static app build with a small pilot group
- Ask for feedback after 3-5 sessions of use
- Collect notes on:
  - Sinhala wording clarity
  - child independence
  - audio usefulness
  - topic difficulty
  - device issues

## Suggested Channels

- Simple Google Form
- WhatsApp feedback from pilot parents
- Teacher interview notes
